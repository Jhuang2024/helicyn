Staged business model

Three proposed stages, in order.

Helicyn is pre-commercial today. The stages below describe the intended path, not current production capability.

Stage 1 · Now

Founding Partner

Current stage. Pre-commercial and relationship-first: product feedback and validation with a small number of partners and operators.

  • Pre-commercial
  • Relationship-first
  • Product feedback and validation
  • Not a paid production product yet
Stage 2 · Future

Commercial Launch

Proposed future stage, once the platform is production-ready.

  • Annual enterprise SaaS/platform subscription
  • Production-ready platform
  • Coordinates workloads, energy, cooling, and infrastructure
Stage 3 · Future

Performance-Based Pricing

Proposed future stage, once savings are verified.

  • Customer keeps most of the verified savings
  • Helicyn receives an agreed percentage of verified savings
Illustrative example · future · only after verified savings $10.0M saved/yr × 15% agreement = $1.5M to Helicyn
Incentive alignment

Helicyn is intended to win only when customers measurably save.

The proposed performance-based stage is designed so Helicyn earns meaningfully only when it generates verified savings in energy, carbon, or cost -- not for unproven promises. Performance pricing requires verification before it applies; this avoids charging customers purely on projected or modeled numbers.
