Patch notes

What's shipped on Helicyn.

Product updates, design changes, performance work, and fixes, in the order they shipped. This is a pre-commercial research preview, so expect steady, incremental changes rather than a fixed release cadence.

Changelog Build v1.0.0 · 2026.07 Research preview
July 3, 2026 Research Latest 1 min read

Published the Helicyn technical report

  • Published the latest Helicyn technical report, including the finalized simulator methodology, parameter table, experimental results, figures, and discussion.
  • The website now links to the current report version across all relevant pages, giving visitors a clearer view of Helicyn's technical thesis and coordination-first approach to data center optimization.
  • Added a dedicated report page and downloadable PDF, and refreshed the research page's abstract, methodology, and findings summaries to match.
July 2, 2026 Fixes 1 min read

Sign-in and email verification fixes

  • Fixed a bug where clicking an account verification or magic-link email could land on a raw error screen instead of your account.
  • Verification and sign-in links now resolve through a single callback page that establishes your session and takes you straight into the partner portal.
  • Expired or already-used links now show a clear explanation and a one-click way to request a new email, instead of a dead end.
June 28, 2026 Fixes 1 min read

General bug fixes and polish

  • Fixed alignment on the founding partner stage rail so the connecting line runs through the center of each stage marker at all three stops.
  • Cleaned up leftover placeholder punctuation across onboarding and research copy in favor of proper commas, colons, and semicolons.
  • Assorted small layout and copy fixes surfaced during the founding partner program build-out.
June 24, 2026 Security 1 min read

Security and infrastructure improvements

  • Tightened Row Level Security policies on founding partner application data so each account can only read or write its own row.
  • Audited client-side configuration handling to confirm no private keys or secrets ship to the browser.
  • General hosting and dependency hardening ahead of wider founding partner onboarding.
June 15, 2026 Growth 1 min read

LinkedIn integration

  • Added a LinkedIn company presence, linked from the site footer and the founding partner pages.
  • Founding partner outreach now runs through one consistent channel between the site and LinkedIn.
June 5, 2026 Product 1 min read

Founding Partner application

  • Shipped the founding partner onboarding form: company details, role, interests, and consent, backed by real per-user storage.
  • Added a partner portal showing live application status (submitted, reviewing, accepted, or waitlisted) instead of a static thank-you page.
  • Added a progress stepper to the onboarding form so partners always know how much is left.
May 22, 2026 Security 1 min read

Authentication system

  • Replaced the placeholder access request form with real accounts: email/password sign up and sign in, plus passwordless magic-link sign-in.
  • Sessions now persist across visits with real refresh-token rotation; signing out actually clears the session everywhere.
  • Every account and application record is backed by Postgres with Row Level Security, not a shared or mocked data store.
May 10, 2026 Design 1 min read

Mobile responsiveness improvements

  • Reworked the primary navigation into a proper mobile menu with a full-height panel and keyboard/escape support.
  • Re-tuned hero, card grid, and form layouts for small screens so nothing truncates or overlaps below 400px wide.
  • Increased tap target sizes across buttons, tabs, and form controls on touch devices.
April 28, 2026 Performance 1 min read

Performance optimizations

  • Deferred and lazy-loaded below-the-fold imagery across the homepage and founding partner pages.
  • Reduced layout thrash in the scroll-triggered reveal animations, especially on lower-end mobile devices.
  • Trimmed unused CSS and consolidated animation keyframes to cut total stylesheet weight.
April 14, 2026 Design 1 min read

Improved animations and UI polish

  • Added a site-wide microinteraction pass: magnetic buttons, cursor-tracked card spotlights, and staggered section reveals.
  • Introduced a reusable illustration and depth system so every section reads as part of one visual system.
  • Replaced placeholder bracket-tag styling on the research page with proper coming-soon panels.
March 30, 2026 Product 1 min read

Interactive Control Plane demo

  • Launched an interactive Control Plane demo simulating multi-region workload, energy, and thermal coordination.
  • Added operator-in-the-loop review: every simulated action can be inspected and accepted or rejected before it applies.
  • Added live status pills and region cards so demo state is legible at a glance, not buried in a log.
March 18, 2026 Design 1 min read

Homepage redesign and visual improvements

  • Rebuilt the homepage hero around a single large logo mark, tagline, and coordination scene instead of a dense wall of copy.
  • Moved explanatory copy into a dedicated overview section with clearer scroll pacing.
  • Added large-scale scroll choreography across the homepage for a more considered, premium feel.
March 2, 2026 Launch 1 min read

Initial public website launch

  • Helicyn's public website is live: homepage, research/thesis overview, and founding partner program pages.
  • Set up the initial static site infrastructure, favicon and metadata, and analytics.
  • This is a pre-commercial research preview; nothing on the site represents a shipped production platform yet.
